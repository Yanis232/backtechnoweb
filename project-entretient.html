Garden Maintenance

Discover the garden maintenance services offered by L'Arbre Envie.

L'Arbre Envie ensures the restoration and/or maintenance of your garden. Below are some of the services offered. A precise and tailored quote based on your needs will be provided during a visit to your garden:

  • Mowing/Brush Clearing
  • Hedge Trimming
  • Maintenance Pruning of Shrubs
  • Manual Weeding (flower beds, walkways, boules court)
  • Creation, restoration, and modification of automatic irrigation (drip or sprinkler)
  • General Cleaning (dead leaves, etc.)
  • Mulching and/or Disposal of Green Waste
  • Soil and Flower Bed Mulching (laying a layer of plant mulch)

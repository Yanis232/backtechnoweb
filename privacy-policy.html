Privacy Policy

Welcome to our website. Your privacy is very important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our site.

1. Information Collection

We collect personal information such as your name, email address, and payment details when you sign up or make a purchase on our site. We also collect non-personal information, such as browsing data and cookie usage.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used to improve your experience on our site, personalize content, send you offers and promotions, and ensure the proper functioning of the site.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless it is necessary for processing your orders or to comply with the law.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to enhance your experience on our site. These cookies are used to analyze site usage and personalize advertisements. You can manage or refuse cookies in your browser settings.

5. Information Security

We take security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access your personal information, correct it, delete it, and object to its processing in certain circumstances. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

7. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at contact@arbre-envie.com.

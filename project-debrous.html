Brush Clearing

Discover the brush clearing services offered by L'Arbre Envie.

L'Arbre Envie performs brush clearing to secure and maintain your land as part of the Legal Obligation of Clearing:

  • Mulching of tall grass, shrubs, and unwanted vegetation
  • Removal of low branches (<2.5m)
  • Selection and removal of trees and shrubs near buildings
  • On-site mulching and spreading of plant material

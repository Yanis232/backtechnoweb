Tree Pruning and Care

Discover the tree pruning and care services offered by L'Arbre Envie.

L'Arbre Envie specializes in tree pruning and care. Tailored solutions for your trees and needs will be provided during a site visit:

  • Pruning and maintenance of tree crowns and branches
  • Thinning and selective pruning
  • Safety pruning and sanitation pruning
  • Tree cabling
  • Pruning for coexistence (fiber cables, roofs, building facades, roadways...)
  • Tree dismantling and/or removal for hazardous or high-risk trees
